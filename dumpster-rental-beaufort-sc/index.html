Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right dumpster size?

Our experts assess your project type and waste volume to recommend the most efficient dumpster size.

What materials are allowed in the dumpster?

Most non‑hazardous materials are fine: wood, drywall, metals, roofing, and furniture. Prohibited items typically include asbestos, liquid chemicals, paint cans with liquid, gas cylinders and medical waste.

Do I need a permit for placement?

If the dumpster sits entirely on your private property (driveway or yard) typically no permit is required. If placement is on a public roadway or sidewalk, local agencies may require a street‑use permit; we can assist in the process if needed.

What happens if the dumpster goes overweight?

Our pricing includes a weight allowance. If you exceed that allowance, we’ll discuss over‑weight fees in advance and offer you options to avoid surprise charges.

How far in advance do I schedule delivery?

For residential jobs, we recommend booking at least 48 hours ahead. For commercial or construction sites, booking one week in advance is wise to secure the size and delivery slot that matches your schedule.

Beaufort, SC Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Beaufort (/ˈbjuːfərt/ BEW-fert, different from that of Beaufort, North Carolina) is a city in and the county seat of Beaufort County, South Carolina, United States. Chartered in 1711, it is the second-oldest city in South Carolina, behind Charleston, which was established in 1670. A city rich in history, culture, southern hospitality, and a seaside charm, Beaufort is affectionally known as the "Queen of the Carolina Sea Islands". The city's population was 13,607 at the 2020 census. It is part of the Hilton Head Island–Bluffton metropolitan area.
